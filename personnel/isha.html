Isha Roushan

Co-founder & Managing Director

Biography

Isha Kanwal is the Managing Director of Roushan Research International. Under her leadership SALOWN School of Hair and Beauty Techonology has reaped the position of becoming India's first awarding and accreditation organization in the filed of hair and beauty industry.

Today SALOWN School of Hair and Beauty Technology offer training and development courses not only for the learners but also works with clients to design their organizational structures, roles and responsibilities. The firm helps organizations and businesses hire the right people and advise them on how to reward, develop and motivate their workforce in the hair and beauty industry. The firm also helps professionals navigate and advance their careers in the area of hair and beauty industry.

Isha brings hands-on experience to her current position. Prior to SALOWN School of Hair and Beauty Technology, Isha has worked with banking majors like HDFC Bank, Axis Bank, AlphaBricks Techonology etc. She is also the co-founder of RezMatic Technology.

Isha Roushan earned her Diploma in Professional Beauty Therapy from Austin Peay State University, US and also a CPD certified professional from UK. Isha also holds a Masters in Business Administration. She is an avid reader, trainer.
