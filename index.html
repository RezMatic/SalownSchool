Awarding Organization

Salown School is an Awarding Organisation (AO) in the field of hair and beauty industry and promotes qualifications, exams and assessments to met the learners and employers expectations.

Rigorous standards

Salown School’s Academic Advisory Board is made up of invited members who provide extensive experience in education, strategy, business management and quality assurances.

Regulated and bespoke qualifications

Our portfolio includes international regulated qualifications as well as bespoke qualifications that include endorsed and certified programmes across hair and beauty sectors internationally.

International scope

Salown School continues its mission to be recognised globally with qualifications that meet the demands of employers and learners both vocationally and academically.

Professional degree pathways

Salown School provides a more flexible, cost effective route for learners to achieve awards, certificates and diplomas through our progression routes.

Quality standards and valid, valued learning.

As an autonomous awarding organisation, Salown School is able to give assurances to employers, centres and learners of consistent, rigorous, quality standards and valid, valued learning. Whether you’re a learner or an employer, competition today is intense. Organisations around the globe are seeking knowledgeable, highly skilled and productive employees. Salown School qualifications provide both learners and employers the recognition of competency that can build employee careers and global enterprises. And our qualifications combine UK and Indian standards with relevant international content, so learners can achieve their full potential in today’s global economy.

TECHNOLOGY FOR ALL


Teachers, Students and Parents connect seemlessly

SALOWN School takes pride in becoming the First Training School in the World in the hair and beauty industry to provide technology for students, parents, teachers and centre administrators. Now trainers and teachers can deliver courses through online classes, organize exams using Proctoring Technology and students can attend classes, take their examinations and submit their assignments seemlessly from anywhere in the world through a single app. The Centre adminstrators can now do more in less time by completing admissions, collecting fees from students and organizing customized remote classes using the SAMS : (SALOWN Academy Management Software), a single platfrom for managing all adminstrative activities and delivering remote classes, examinations and much more. On the other hand students can access all the course material, take their exams securely from anywhere, see their upcoming class schedules and much more from the SAMS app which can be easily accessible on their desktop as well as through our own mobile app available on iOS and Android smartphones.
